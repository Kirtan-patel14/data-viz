My Interests

Technology is one of my biggest interests because of the impact it has on nearly every aspect of modern life. From communication to healthcare to finance, software and data systems help people make better decisions and solve complex problems. Learning how these systems work and how they are built has been a major motivation for me in studying computer science.

Another area that interests me is data visualization. Data on its own can be difficult to interpret, but visualization tools allow people to quickly identify patterns, trends, and relationships. Using programming languages and visualization libraries, it is possible to transform large datasets into clear and engaging graphics.

I am also interested in financial data analysis. Understanding how markets behave and analyzing economic trends can reveal valuable insights about the world. Combining finance with programming and data visualization creates exciting opportunities for developing analytical tools and dashboards.
